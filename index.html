Introduction

Michael Haneke is known to some as the greatest filmmaker alive. Since premiering his first film in 1989, Haneke has become an international auteur in cinema. The director is one of a small group of filmmakers to win the Palm d'Or twice for his films The White Ribbon and Amour.
